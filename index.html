Supply Chain Q&A

We source our timber from sustainably managed forests across Central and Eastern Europe. Every log is fully traceable — from the forest of origin to our production facilities. We maintain long-term partnerships with certified forest managers in Romania, Finland, and Ukraine, ensuring responsible forestry practices.
Sustainability is built into every stage of our operations. We hold FSC and PEFC chain-of-custody certifications, conduct regular third-party audits, and invest in forest regeneration programs. Our annual sustainability report details our carbon footprint reduction targets and reforestation efforts.
Our products carry FSC and PEFC chain-of-custody certifications, CE marking for structural timber, and meet EN 14081 grading standards. Specific products also carry fire resistance ratings and additional national certifications depending on the destination market.
Standard products are available within 5–10 business days for European markets. Intercontinental shipments typically require 4–6 weeks. We offer just-in-time delivery schedules for large construction projects with pre-negotiated volumes.
Yes. Our manufacturing facilities are equipped for custom cutting, planing, and profiling. Minimum order quantities apply for custom specifications. Contact our sales team for a detailed quote — we typically turn around custom orders within 15–20 business days.
